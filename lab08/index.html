Cheeseburger Recipe


How to Make a Cheeseburger

You'll need the following ingredients:

  • Cheese (your choice on which kind you use)
  • Beef (at least 20% fat to ensure juiciness)
  • Two slices of bread or one bun

How to prepare a cheeseburger:

  1. Preheat your grill or oven top. It's recommended that you heat your burger to 160-165Â°F.
  2. When the grill/oven is heated enough, place the beef patty onto the pan.
  3. After around seven minutes, flip the patty over. You may have to wait longer depending on the size of the patty.
  4. When the burger is close to ready, place a slice of cheese onto the patty.
  5. Once the patty is heated to preference on both sides, remove it from the pan and place it onto the bread.
  6. Turn off the grill/oven and set the pan aside to cool down.
