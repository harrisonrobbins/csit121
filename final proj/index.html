Note: Due to the size of the data collected for this project, as well as time constraints, only a few players and images will be displayed in this version of the website. I will eventually go back and insert all the other players in future editions of this site, but that won't be hosted here.

Research for this website was conducted with the help of Pro Football Reference. It was pretty tedious combing through thousands of NFL players to collect the necessary data, but it was also kinda fun because it introduced me to some players that I would've likely never heard of before.
